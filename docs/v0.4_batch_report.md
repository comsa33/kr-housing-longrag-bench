## 8. Limitations (honest)

1. **Single provider / limited 시·도 spread.** All 10 announcements are LH; 8/10 are 경기도. The QA-level
   single-announcement dominance is fine (14.9%), but source-level provider/region diversity is thin.
   v0.5 should add SH/GH/기타 공공기관 announcements and more 시·도.
2. **Table coverage.** MOLIT is 6 자치구 (서울 4 + 대전 2), 2025 only; constituent-구 cross-source is
   only available for 대전 동구. Other announcements cross-source against HUG at the 시·도 level.
3. **Extraction noise.** `pdftotext -layout` duplicates some column text (e.g., "청주지북지구 청주지북지구").
   Grounding is whitespace-insensitive and gold_terms are short contiguous fragments, so this does not
   affect correctness, but page text is not a clean reading order.
4. **Statute effective dates.** Statutes were captured at their v0.2 efYd (2015/2018) while announcements
   are 2024–2026; cross_document_legal items target provisions that are stable across those versions, but
   a future pass should pin current-law versions.
5. **Agent-authored share.** 17.5% of QA is agent-authored; these passed adversarial review + grounding
   but await human sign-off (§7).
